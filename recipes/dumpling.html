Piping hot dumpling!

Description

Below are the ingredients and instructions needed to make the fried seafood wonton!

Ingredients

Main ingredients

  • 3 pieces scallions
  • 21 oz wonton wrappers

Shrimp ingredients

  • 6 oz shrimp
  • 1.5 tsp cornstarch

Pork ingredients

  • 18 oz pork shoulder
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 0.75 tsp white pepper
  • 1.5 tsp sugar
  • 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1.5 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 1.5 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Chop scallions
  2. Prepare pork
  3. Chop and marinate shrimp
  4. Squeeze and marinate pork
  5. Refrigerate filling
  6. Mix filling
  7. Fold wontons
  8. Freezing wontons(optional)
  9. Boil wontons
  10. Plate and enjoy!
